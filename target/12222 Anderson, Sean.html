Biog

Tall left-sided Dundonian youngster who joined the U17 squad in 2004-05. Featured in central midfield, out wide or in central defence as he progressed to the U19 team in 2006-07.

Released and went to boyhood favourites Dundee United to play in their U19s 2007-08. Freed by Levein and taken on by East Stirlingshire. Played alongside other new players like Dean Richardson in 2008-09 and helped the Shire shake off their joke team tag, for the first time in many a long year.

Opted to move nearer to home with Montrose ahead of 2009-10, but the move turned sour and he switched to Lochee United Juniors in January 2010.

Returned to Shire ahead of 2010-11, where his versatility and quality is rated highly by manager Jim McInally.
