A to Z of Celtic Players

Do you want to add a player or biography?
If you want to add a player to this list, it couldn't be easier - navigate to the appropriate letter for the start of the player's surname and simply press EasyEdit at the top of the page and their name into the list.

If you are feeling creative, you may wish to add some pictures, or even a whole biography of a former player. To do this, click on 'Add a New Page' on the right and start typing away. When you're finished, just come back here, click EasyEdit, select the name of the players for whom you've just completed some words for and use the 'Link' button that appears at the top to link your new page to the player.
