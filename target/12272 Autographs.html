These are some Autographs I got 1 day at Glasgow Airport( circa 1978ish???)
Was just a wee lad, & the Celtic team were playing away in Europe, stood there shocked to see my heroes, until big Billy said,
"nice that your going on holiday son, were off to work!"
He then got the rest of the players that were hanging about to sign there autographs for me!
The autographs are moving top row L to R and then downwards - Roy Aitken,Davie Provan,Tom McAdam,Peter Latchford,Billy McNeill,Vic Davidson,Frank McGarvey,Brian Scott,John Clark,Danny McGrain and the last two together are Bobby Lennox and Johnny Doyle.
