This four team tournament was staged in June 1985 at the B.C. Place - a recently build 60,000 superstadium in Vancouver with an air-supported dome roof.

Celtic were invited to take part alongside Dutch giants Ajax and local entrants Pacific Rim Soccer League All-Stars and North Shore Colts. The Bhoys progressed to the final by defeating the All-Stars 2-1. In the final the Celts went down 2-1 to the Colts in front of a crowd of just under 10,000.
