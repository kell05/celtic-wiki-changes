Lyrics

#Ohhhhh Artur Boruc,
The Holy Goalie,
He hates the huns!!

He blessed himself at Ibrox and the Huns went off their nut,
he's off his fecking rocket and sings:
GOD BLESS THE POPE#

(Repeat ad infinitum)


Background

Tune is based on the back of a Liverpool tune, our version is based after Boruc was ridiculously cautioned for having crossed himself at Ibrox even though its something he always does, and in any case can hardly be stated to be inflammatory. It's been a continuing battle for Boruc, and why he should be told to hide his show of faith (hardly overt in most cases) is just ridiculous.

Became quite an iconic tune back during Boruc's tenure, and really cemented his cult status.
