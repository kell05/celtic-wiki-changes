Celtic Career Review
Never played a competitive first-team match for Celtic, after signing from Lochore Welfare Juniors on 19 October 1963.

Released to Alloa two years later and had four years with 'the wasps' before heading to England and three years with Southern League professionals, Cambridge City. A short spell with Bedford Town followed, before a return to his native Fife with Dunfermline in 1973. Finished his senior career with Berwick Rangers in 1974-75.
