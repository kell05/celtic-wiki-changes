Celtic Career Review
A bustling forward who never played a competitive match for Celtic.

When released by Celtic he joined Clyde going on to have a good first season with them. 2005-06 was a poor season and he started 06/07 out of contract and playing as a 'Trialist' before being offered a further 1 year deal and ending that season as Clyde's top scorer. He was released by Clyde in July 2008.

After a trial at Queen of the South he was offered a 1 year deal. He broke his leg playing against Morton in January 2009. Queen of the South released him at the end of 2008/09.

He is currently without a club.

Back to fitness, he was spotted on trial at Ayr United in July 2010. Played in "the Honestmen's" season opener but moved on. Linked up with Airdrie United on trial in the second week of September. He signed for Arthurlie Juniors in October 2010.
